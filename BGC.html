Description of College

On 12 May 2016, Chittagong Govt. Commercial Institute was recognized as Bakalia Government College as per the notification of the Ministry of Education and the Department of Education. From then on the path of this college. At present, the college has 36 teachers in the science, humanities and business education departments. It is officially the 6th college in Chittagong.
